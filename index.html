Our Practice

A return to Indiana, rooted in care.

Mahony-Rathburn Chiropractic is a hands-on, full spinal chiropractic practice located in Fort Wayne's north side — near Cedar Creek Church, Metea State Park, and just 8 minutes from I-69 and Parkview Regional Medical Center.

Dr. Orion Mahony and Dr. Kami Rathburn met at Palmer College of Chiropractic, built a thriving practice together on Orcas Island, Washington, and eventually made the journey back home to Indiana to be near family — and to serve a community they care about.

We don't rush you in and out. Every patient gets real attention, honest answers, and a care plan that fits their life.

Services & specialties

We treat patients of all ages — from active teens and athletes to adults managing chronic pain and those focused on long-term wellness.

Back & Neck Pain

Full spinal adjustments for acute and chronic pain, disc issues, and postural strain — using hands-on techniques tailored to each patient.

Acute Injuries

Auto accidents, work injuries, sports injuries — we provide early, effective care to support recovery and reduce long-term complications.

Dr. Mahony & Dr. Rathburn

TMJ (Jaw Pain)

Addressing jaw clicking, locking, and related headaches through gentle, targeted adjustment and soft tissue work.

Dr. Mahony & Dr. Rathburn

Sports & Teen Athletes

Helping young athletes recover from sports injuries, improve performance, and stay in the game longer.

Dr. Mahony & Dr. Rathburn

Wellness & Maintenance

Ongoing chiropractic care for patients who want to stay well, move better, and prevent issues before they start.

Dr. Mahony & Dr. Rathburn

Upper-Cervical Specific

Precise, gentle adjustments to the top of the spine — often powerful for headaches, migraines, and neurological symptoms.

Dr. Mahony & Dr. Rathburn

Graston Technique

A specialized soft tissue therapy using stainless steel instruments to break up scar tissue, reduce chronic inflammation, and restore function — particularly effective for tendinopathy, plantar fasciitis, and repetitive strain.

Meet the doctors

Palmer College graduates, Orcas Island veterans, and Indiana homecomers — Drs. Mahony and Rathburn bring warmth and expertise to every visit.

Dr. Orion Mahony

D.C. · 21 Years Experience

Dr. Mahony treats musculoskeletal issues, acute injuries, TMJ, knees, back and neck pain, and is especially skilled with teens and sports-related conditions. He also practices maintenance and wellness chiropractic, and upper-cervical specific care. He trained at Palmer College of Chiropractic and spent 15+ years building a practice on Orcas Island before returning to Indiana.

Back & Neck TMJ Sports Upper-Cervical Wellness Knees Acute Injuries

Dr. Kami Rathburn

B.S., D.C. · Palmer College of Chiropractic

Born and raised in Indiana, Dr. Rathburn earned her B.S. in Biology from Bowling Green State University and graduated from Palmer College of Chiropractic in 2000. A former collegiate cross country runner, she brings an athlete's understanding to patient care — and is the practice's certified Graston practitioner. Currently pursuing advanced graduate studies.
